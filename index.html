A review of Kawangware

Kawangware is slum in nairobi located exactly 15km west of nairobi

kawangware is one of the major slums in E.A having a population of over 650,000 people 60% being youth and children

kawangware is famously known for the local TV sow known as the REAL HOUSE HELPS OF KAWANGWARE which featured kenyan celebrities such as DJ Shiti,Njugush and many others

Apart from the slum part of it,Kawangware is also developing in terms of infrastructure and construction of mordern houses

The local governmment with patnership with the national government are working in hand to ensure that social amenities are achieved in the area

Being a densily populated area Kawangware has faces some challenges such as;

  • High crime rates
  • Increased rates of unemployment
  • Poor housing leading to development of slums
